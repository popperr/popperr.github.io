Lithium Soliton

On the lithium experiment, we explore the nonlinear dynamics of atomic solitons and their quantum nature on a large mass scale. When lithium 7 is cooled to quantum degeneracy in a quasi-1D magnetic trap, it forms a soliton, or self-reinforcing solitary wave, within the trap.
